Whale Clock

Wake up to a singing whale.

How it works

  1. Tap the displayed time and choose when you want to wake up.
  2. Switch the alarm to ON.
  3. Allow alarms when iOS asks. Whale Clock will sound every day at your chosen time.

Troubleshooting

If the alarm cannot be enabled, open Settings, choose Apps, then Whale Clock, and allow alarms. Whale Clock requires iOS 26 or later.
