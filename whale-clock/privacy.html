Whale Clock Privacy Policy

Last updated July 20, 2026

Our approach

Whale Clock does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The app contains no advertising, analytics, tracking technology, accounts, or third-party software development kits.

Information on your device

Your chosen wake-up time is saved locally on your device so Whale Clock can remember it. The app uses Apple’s AlarmKit system to request alarm permission and schedule the alarm. This information is not sent to Burning Flowers.

Deleting your information

You can switch the alarm off at any time. Deleting Whale Clock removes the app and its locally stored preferences.

Children’s privacy

Because Whale Clock does not collect personal data from anyone, it does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the revised policy and its updated date will be posted on this page.
